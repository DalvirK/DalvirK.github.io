Paneer Paratha

A very delicious Indian food! When I traveled to India my brothers and I always ordered theses at resturants. It is two rotis joined with paneer filled inside. It takes marvelous!

Ice Cream

It's a hot day, the first thing that comes to mind is ice cream! I especially love icecream with any types of nuts or chips in it. Oreo, vanilla (with almonds and or sprinkles), choclate chip and cookies and cream are my favorite.

Burritos

On Sunday nights (most sundays) my mom, my sister and I get together to make burritos. For the meat we substitute black beans. The minimum we put in them is lettuce, beans, spanish rice, onions, cheese, tomatoes, cilantro, and guacamole. To make it even better we add corn. It is delicious!
