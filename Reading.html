My favorite books

Harry Potter

Harry Potter was one of those books I could not put down. I took it into the restroom, I took it to bed, if I could I would take it in my shower. It was that good. The books are better than the writing.

Artimes Fowl

Artimes Fowl is an amazing book. I always found myself rooting for the hero even though I usually pick the villians side. The main character is an anti-hero and many people dig that. It is about a boy fighting against evil fairies, when himself isn't that good either.

Alex Rider

What kid didn't want to be part of a super secret spy operation? It was every kids dream. It was mine too and Alex Rider lives it for you. A great book with the life a teenage boy turned spy with the devil's luck.

Excape from Furnace Books

This book is insane. A boy falsely accused of murder is trying planning to escape the prison, only if he survives. Monsters are brewing underneath the prison. It is awesome!

Charlie Bone

Kid's superpower reading pictures. And that is how he is going to try to save the world. This is a great book, not gory at all great for all ages. I really loved this book.

To Kill a Mockingbird

This book ripped out my heart. It is beautifully written and so realistic. I love this author and the main character. Please read it, it is a classic.
