What is Sikhi?

  • Originated from Punjab, India

    Sikhism was founded by Sri Guru Nanak Dev ji in 1469 Punjab, India. Sikh means 'disciple' or student. 16 million people in punjab practice it today.

  • It is the 6th largest religion

    Sikhi is now practiced all over the globe. Sikhs believe everyone is equal and that they should serve people in need.

  • Believe in One universal God

    Sikhs believe in Sri Guru Granth Sahib ji, who is currently the eleventh guru. They are a monotheistic religion, so they worship one god.

The three Golden Truths

These 3 rules truths were written by Sri Guru Nanak Dev ji. We as Sikhs do our best to follow these in our daily lives.

  • Naam Jappo. Always remember God. We try to remember our lord during our day.
  • Wannd CChako. Share one's earnings with others.
  • Kirat Karo. Work hard and honestly.
