Gurudwara (Sikh Temple)

A gurudwara is a sikh place of worship. The word means 'the residence of the guru'. A gurudwara can be reconized from a far because of the Nishan Sahib. At a gurudwara there is a langar were free food is given. Our eleventh guru, Sri Guru Granth Sahib ji resides in the darbar sahib.
