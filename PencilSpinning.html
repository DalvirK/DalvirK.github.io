The Big Three

Three things that are the most important in my life are listed below. I give these the most priority in my life. They are not in any particular order.

  • My Community

    If I could say one place other than home where I have spent most of my life it would be at my Gurudwara (sikh temple). I am connected with my community and I am really grateful for them.

  • My Family

    I am an introvert, most of my family is an introvert. I am best friends with my sisters and brothers. We are a big family so we bump heads a lot and we are always stuck together.

  • My Future

    This is a bit vague, but my future mostly means school (career), and who I want to be in five to ten years (personality/skills). I constantly try to ask myself, am I growing or stuck in the same place I was 5 years ago?
