Pencil Spinning

It is a skill that looks really cool. I only started a while ago and only know two tricks. Above is video is people who are really good at it. Below are reasons for taking this up as a hobby.

  • Self Entertaining

    If you are bored and have nothing to do with your hands, pencil spin! It is a great distraction, and with practice you can do it without thinking.

  • It looks super cool

    If you haven't watched the video above watch it. Then come back and read this again. It is super cool, and can be tranferred on other objects.

  • Competitions and community

    There are competitions held for pencil spinning and meetings too. If you want to join start learning. There are many videos on youtube!
