Longboarding

I started longboarding in middle school. It was very hard for me at first, balancing and accelerating at the same time. I used to fall a lot because we had so many small rocks everywhere. Longboarding in my opinion is easier to learn than skatboarding. It really smooth and feels amazing to ride. Longboards are different than skating, as they are longer and wider. This also makes it easier to learn. Skateboards are more for doing tricks while Longboards are not. They are very fun to ride and relaxing.
