Gatka, Sikh martial art

Originated in Punjab, India in the 15th century. It is played most by Nihang Singhs (warriors in Punjab).

California Gatka Dal

I am part of California Gatka Dal. This group contains all Gatka players across California. There are Gatka players in Stockton, Fremont, El sobrante, Fresno (where I am from) and Riverside that I know. They are very welcoming and kind.

Jaur-e-Teg and YUDH

These two competetions are held in the fall and players across the states play against each other in teams of three. I played this year in Jaur e teg, and I am going to play in YUDH soon. It is a game were we fight each other with sotia or sticks. Similar to fencing.

Riverside Holla Mohalla

This is a competition held in March in Riverside CA. Teams of 16 people compete against eachother with ther performance. The food is great there.
